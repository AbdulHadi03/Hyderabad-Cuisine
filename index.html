Most prominent Cuisine

Hyderabadi cuisine has different recipes for different events, and hence is categorized accordingly, from banquet food, for weddings and parties, festival foods, and travel foods. The legacy of authentic Hyderabadi cuisine and its unabridged royal saga of dishes and recipes is believed to still persist with old Deccan families.
